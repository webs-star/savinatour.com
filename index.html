Our Services

Masai Mara Safari

Embark on a thrilling adventure through the Masai Mara, home to the iconic Big Five. Witness the Great Migration and enjoy unforgettable game drives across the open savannah. Elevate your experience with a sunrise hot air balloon ride over the plains.

Mt. Kenya Hiking

Conquer Kenya’s tallest peak with expert-guided treks through forested slopes and alpine meadows. Marvel at rare flora and crystal-clear mountain streams as you ascend. Enjoy breathtaking views from Point Lenana and feel on top of Africa.

Beaches Safari Holidays

Relax on Kenya's pristine beaches, where turquoise waters meet golden sands. Combine coastal leisure with wildlife excursions for a unique safari experience. Stay in beachfront hotels offering stunning ocean views and exceptional hospitality. Visit Lake Nakuru National Park, renowned for its vibrant flocks of flamingos and diverse wildlife. Explore the scenic landscapes and spot rhinos, giraffes, and other species. Capture the mesmerizing sight of pink-hued shores teeming with birdlife.

Kenya Lodges Safari and Rich Kenya Culture

Immerse yourself in Kenya's rich cultural heritage by staying in traditional lodges. Engage with local communities, experience Maasai traditions, and enjoy authentic cuisine. These lodges offer comfort while providing insights into Kenya's diverse cultures.

Switzerland Adventure Packages

Harder Kulm

Ride the funicular to Harder Kulm, Interlaken's local mountain, for breathtaking vistas of Lake Thun and Lake Brienz. Dine at the panoramic restaurant and capture stunning photographs from the viewing platform. A must-visit for nature lovers..

Beatus Caves & Beatenburg

Explore the mystical St. Beatus Caves, nestled in the cliffs above Lake Thun. Wander through illuminated chambers adorned with stalactites and stalagmites. Learn about the legend of Saint Beatus and the dragon.Discover the tranquil village of Beatenberg, offering panoramic views of the Bernese Alps. Combine your visit with an exploration of the nearby St. Beatus Caves. Experience the perfect blend of nature and legend.

Grindelwald

Visit the charming alpine village of Grindelwald, a gateway to the Jungfrau region. Enjoy outdoor activities like hiking, skiing, and mountain biking amidst stunning scenery. Relax in cozy chalets and savor Swiss hospitality.

Schynige Platte

Take a nostalgic ride on the cogwheel train to Schynige Platte, renowned for its alpine gardens and hiking trails. Admire panoramic views of the Eiger, Mönch, and Jungfrau peaks. A paradise for nature enthusiasts..

Paragliding, skydiving & Skiing

Experience the thrill of paragliding over the Swiss Alps or skiing down pristine slopes and skydiving. Interlaken and surrounding regions offer excellent facilities for both activities. Adventure seekers will find their paradise here..

Adventure Tours: Jungfrajoch,Jet Boat, Kayak

Engage in a variety of adrenaline-pumping activities in Switzerland's adventure capital. From jungfrajoch to jet boat rides on alpine lakes, there's something for everyone. Try canyoning, kayaking for unforgettable memories.
